## Use Case: Gastroenteritis Symptom Self-Assessment

### User Pain Points:
- Confusion about whether symptoms need a doctor's visit.
- Lack of clarity on safe foods and fluids during recovery.
- Risk of dehydration, especially in seniors and kids.

### Chatbot Goals:
- Provide simple symptom self-check support.
- Share safe hydration and nutrition advice.
- Guide users when to seek medical attention.

### Success Criteria:
- Users understand dehydration signs.
- Users learn safe at-home care practices.
- Clear ethical disclaimer provided: not for diagnosis.
